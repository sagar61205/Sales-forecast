<h4>📊 Business Problem Statement:</h4>
<p>The client faced significant challenges in pharmaceutical supply chain management:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Lack of visibility into future demand made it impossible to optimize procurement from suppliers.</li>
  <li>Missed revenue opportunities due to the inability to identify and capitalize on sales trends.</li>
</ul>

<h4>📊 My role and the solution:</h4>
<p>As the Data Scientist, I was tasked with building an automated, data-driven forecasting system. I designed and deployed an end-to-end machine learning pipeline that predicted future sales with high accuracy, enabling the team to make smarter, proactive inventory decisions.</p>

<h5>Key Objectives:</h5>
<ul>
  <li>Trends analysis: Identify trends and seasonality in sales data over a 10 year period.</li>
  <li>Accurate Forecasting: Build a robust forecasting model for the next year.</li>
  <li>Supplier Behavior: Provide actionable insights on supplier performance to optimize inventory management and competitive intelligence.</li>
</ul>

<h4>Business Impact:</h4>
<ul>
  <li>+5.3% Sales Increase through optimized inventory management</li>
  <li>Actionable Supplier Insights: Enabled strategic negotiations with top-performing suppliers</li>
  <li>Operational Efficiency: Automated previously manual forecasting processes</li>
  <li>Inventory Optimization: Reduced stockouts and overstock situations through accurate forecasting</li>
  <li>Competitive Intelligence: Enabled data-driven inventory planning and competitive intelligence</li>
</ul>

<h4>Technical Implementation</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Data Architecture: Processed millions of records to classify, restructure, and prepare time-series data</li>
  <li>Model Experimentation: Tested on ARIMA and SARIMAX</li>
  <li>Optimal Model Selection: ARIMA delivered best results for this specific use case</li>
  <li>Error Reduction: Minimized forecast error from 10-15% range to 4-6% meeting business requirements</li>
</ul>
